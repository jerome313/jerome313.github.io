About

English For Everyone

Hi!, I am Maria Romel, Trainer for IELTS, TOEFL or PTE based in Pune Camp., Maharashtra, India. This is an interactive platform for my current and past students to give their feedback and interact with each other, share their knowledge and experience about the training as well as exams.

For the past ten years, I have trained and guided students to score their desired band successfully for these international exams. Besides these exams, I have also coached students for English Language course for other competitive exams such as CAT, GRE, UPSC Bank exams and Spoken English and have conducted Business Communication classes for colleges and IT industry. A personal trainer, with limited number of students in each batch, individuals receive step-by-step guidance to comprehend each module, which includes in-depth grammar lessons and proven techniques with opportunity to solve numerous tests. At present, I train exclusively for IELTS , TOEFL and PTE Exams.

A degree in Economics(Hons.) and Dip. In Business Management, together with work experience in Manufacturing industry in Management cadre, helps me to understand the core abilities of my students and encourage them to overcome challenges confidently. I have developed a special affinity to train students from vernacular schools, who have been my star performers. By experience, I have observed that to a great extent, success of students depend on their lack of confidence or overconfidence and so my lessons aim to improve not only their English Language skills, but, also mould their personality, so that they can be successful in all spheres of life. Goodluck to all my students!
